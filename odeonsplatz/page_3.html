Discover Odeonsplatz

Great job,
you shirker!

You honored the name of this street! Those who wanted to abstain from the Nazi salute, used to walk through Viscardigasse. For this reason, Viscardigasse was renamed “Drückebergergasse” (Shirker’s Alley).

Look down: Do you notice anything particular?

Those golden cobblestones are “Argumente”, a memorial designed by the artist Bruno Wank to celebrate the resistance of the many who walked this street.
