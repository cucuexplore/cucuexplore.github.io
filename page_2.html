Did you Know?

After the Nazis took power in 1933, the Feldherrnhalle became a symbol for the Nazi Propaganda. Indeed, Hitler turned this monument into a memorial to the Nazis killed during the failed Putsch
10 years before.

The monument was constantly guarded by a SS guard, and anyone who passed by
had to hail the site with the Nazi salute.
